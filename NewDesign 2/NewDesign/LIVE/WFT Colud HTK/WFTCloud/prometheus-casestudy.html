Case Study

Challenges

The customer is a US based enterprise application software company specialized in Enterprise Asset Management software that delivers SAP solutions for customers in manufacturing industry worldwide.

Constantly striving for high standard and out-of-box solutions in their applications, the customer needed a well-managed and supported environment from development, quality assurance, pre-production and production. The challenges they faced were:

  • Minimum in-house SAP Basis and infrastructure resources to manage the landscape
  • Long provisioning time for additional SAP systems
  • High-cost on the existing cloud hosting provider without SAP support service
  • Slow Performance on the existing cloud hosting provider

They were in need of a service provider not just hosting their SAP landscape in cloud but also providing SAP basis management that meet their SLAs and ROT/RPO requirements.

Solutions

  • Implement backup and restore solution
  • End-to-end support for any SAP Basis activities and infrastructure requirements
  • Provide monitoring support with helpdesk service 24X7X365
  • Provide SAP maintenance patch and version upgrades, OS and database upgrades, SAP system copy, etc.
  • Maintain SLA agreements and RTO/RPO requirements
  • Configure end-to-end alert management system
