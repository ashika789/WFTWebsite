WFT WFT Announces the Opening of India Office in Chennai
July 16, 2013

Wharfedale Technologies Inc., (WFT Cloud) a global leader in enterprise SAP Cloud solutions, is proud to announce the opening of their India office in Chennai, Tamilnadu. The office will be a key hub for expanding the firm's SAP footprint in India and Asia as well as a key driver of cost efficiency for new and existing enterprise customers globally.

Princeton, NJ (PRWEB) July 16, 2013

Wharfedale Technologies, Inc. announced today the opening of their India office in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The office will be a key hub for expanding the firm’s SAP footprint in India and Asia as well as a key driver of cost efficiency for new and existing enterprise customers globally. It will further serve as an innovation hub and will underpin the firm’s strategy of deploying innovative SAP infrastructure solutions including virtualization and cloud computing.

The center aims to bring innovation to clients by combining SAP’s most advanced technologies (such as leveraging SAP HANA to improve business performance and drive growth and profitability) with WFT’s decade-long field experience in deploying SAP infrastructure solutions at multiple Fortune 500 companies.

Arun Kumar Palanisamy, Client Partner, WFT India Operations, stated, “Our extensive portfolio of consulting, application management, and technical management services, complemented by experienced technical resources, gives us the ability to meet all the needs of the SAP on Cloud customer ecosystem.“

The expanded presence in India will also allow WFT to increase its level of service to clients and partners across Asia and deliver more advanced and high-value solutions. Key clients in India span multiple sectors including telecommunications, electronics and government.

To support its business growth in the country, WFT Cloud is working with a number of universities to help cultivate local technology talent, as well as offering training programs on the latest technologies such ascloud computing and HANA.

The team in the India office will further leverage WFT’s proprietary cloud computing methodology to deliver benefits to clients seeking to reduce their SAP operational management cost by seamlessly moving their SAP to cloud at an affordable price.

“We offer a low per-user monthly cost model,” stated by Ram Prakash, Client Partner, WFT India Operations, “Which does not involve initial investment and helps optimize the IT budget throughout the SAP life cycle, while also reducing costs associated withmaintenance and support.”

About Wharfedale Technologies Inc.

Wharfedale Technologies Inc. (WFT Cloud) is a leading technology consulting firm specializing in SAP infrastructure integrations and services, as well as private/public hybrid Cloud solutions. As an SAP certified provider of cloud services, Wharfedale also offers SAP solutions around - high availability, disaster recovery, advanced sizing for virtualization, back up/recovery Integration, systems refresh automation and landscape optimization.

For more information on Wharfedale and WFT Cloud services, please visit http://www.wftcloud.com or call us at (888) 533-3113.
